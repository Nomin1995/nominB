H&M

H&M (Hennes & Mauritz) is a global fashion retailer that sells clothing, accessories, footwear, cosmetics, and home textiles. The company was founded in 1947 by Erling Persson in Västerås, Sweden, and is headquartered in Stockholm. H&M is known for its fast fashion business model and affordable prices

We are a global family of brands and businesses. We make it possible for customers around the world to express themselves through fashion and design in a sustainable way.
